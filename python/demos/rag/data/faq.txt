Return Information
What is the return policy for Exercise Equipment Limited?
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you may return the item within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange, provided the item is in its original condition and packaging. Please bring your receipt or proof of purchase to process the return.

Are there any items that cannot be returned?
For hygiene and safety reasons, certain items such as opened fitness accessories, personal care products, and custom orders are non-returnable. Please check with our customer service team for specific details.
